Click-n-Drive

The Click-n-Drive system is a very popular accessory among MINI drivers, especially as our phones have become more and more useful in the car. Being designed by MINI themselves, the system integrates well with the gauge cluster providing an OEM look and feel. The system neatly folds away when not in use and initially held the most common devices securely.

However, as phones have grown larger, the Click-n-Drive system's universal holder has become incompatible with most of today's devices. Further the original device holder was not the most convenient solution. Today We prefer magnetic attachments that offer the ultimate convenience. Our holders are also independent of any device size constraints and can hold just about any size of device, even small tablets, so as device trends change, you don't need to worry about losing compatibility.

Each of these attachments is designed to be compatible with MINI's Click-n-Drive system. We offer a lifetime warranty on each attachment. In our experience, the Click-n-Drive system is not ideal for today's larger phones. These magnetic attachments offer wider compatibility, and a better user expeerience.

MagSafe Holder

Out of the box, this attachment is compatible with all MagSafe devices. Additionally, the holder comes with an adhesive backed metal ring that you can stick to a phone or case to gain compatibility with non-MagSafe devices. The benefit to this option for non-MagSafe devices is that wireless charging is still available when using the adhesive metal ring.

MagSafe Charger Holder

This attachment is designed to simply hold Apple's MagSafe wireless charger, for convenient charging on the go. Charger not included.

Magnet Holder

This is our most secure attachment for non-MagSafe devices. It includes an adhesive backed steel plate that you can stick to your phone or case. Wireless charging is not possible with this version.
