How to make sense of your Mazda generation

  • Mazda chassis codes typically begin with a letter followed by three or four digits
  • The letter often represents the model line (e.g., 'D' for Demio, 'M' for Mazda3)
  • The numbers signify the generation or specific variant of the model
  • Some chassis codes may also include additional characters to denote engine or transmission types
  • These codes are used internally for development, production, and service purposes
