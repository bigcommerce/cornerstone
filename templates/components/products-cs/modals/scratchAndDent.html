Scratch and Dent?

Occasionaly we get new parts or returns that have cosmetic blemishes that do no affect the functionality of the part. Rather than toss them in the scrap bin, we offer the parts for sale at a deeply discounted price. In many cases the location of the blemish prevents it from even being seen once the part is installed. Like all of our products, we still guarantee it to be the correct fit and to work properly. If you do have a fitment issue, we will correct it just as we would with a brand new part. Please note that we do not issue refunds for scratch and dent parts.
